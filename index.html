Weatherly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026 · Publisher: AFTERTIME Media Company · Contact: giftstuff2014@gmail.com

Weatherly is a free weather app. We designed it to need as little of your data as possible. This policy explains what the app accesses and why.

No account, no tracking

Weatherly has no sign-up or login. We do not run analytics, we do not show ads, we do not use tracking SDKs, and we never sell or share your personal data.

Location

If you allow it, the app uses your device location to show local weather and air quality. Your coordinates are sent only as part of requests to the weather data providers listed below, to fetch the forecast for that spot. We do not store your location on any server of ours. You can decline the permission and add cities manually instead; you can revoke it any time in your system settings.

Data stored on your device

Your saved cities, unit and language preferences, and wellness-diary entries are stored only locally on your device. They are never uploaded to us or anyone else. Uninstalling the app removes them.

Third-party services

To show weather the app requests data from these providers, sending only a location (coordinates) and no personal identifiers:

  • Open-Meteo — weather, forecast, air quality, geocoding (terms)
  • RainViewer — precipitation radar tiles (api)
  • NOAA SWPC — planetary Kp index (geomagnetic), no location sent
  • OpenWeather — temperature/wind map tiles (privacy)
  • OpenStreetMap — base map tiles (privacy)

Notifications

If you enable alerts, notifications are generated locally on your device from the forecast. We do not operate a push server and do not send you marketing.

Children

Weatherly does not knowingly collect any personal data and is suitable for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above.
